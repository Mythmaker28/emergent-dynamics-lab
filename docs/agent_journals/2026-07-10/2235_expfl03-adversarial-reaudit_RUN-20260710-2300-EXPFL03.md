## Objections raised (each attempts to explain the EXP-FL-02 positive WITHOUT constituent-carried organization)

O1. **Seed-luck / single-seed inference.** EXP-FL-02 audited 4/4 laws on ONE causal seed (9501). On 12 unseen seeds
    per law the audited rate is **2/46 = 4.3% [Wilson 95% CI 1.2%-14.5%]**. The original 4/4 is not reproducible.
    **SUSTAINED — this alone withdraws the positive.**

O2. **Trivial coherence (frozen lump).** A displaced coherent lump can persist at the new site by translation-
    covariance without being a throughput-sustaining organization. Adding the CONTINUED-TURNOVER requirement shows
    this is the DOMINANT reality: **30/46 (65%) of enrolled units re-establish, beat the placebo, leave the old
    site unregenerated -- and then STOP exchanging constituents.** Without this criterion I would have reported
    ~32/46 "successes" and been wrong. **SUSTAINED — this is the same failure that killed CORE V0 law 52.**

O3. **Observer artefact.** The 2 surviving audited units are cadence-dependent: at cadence 5 they become
    frozen_lump / destroyed; at cadence 20 destroyed / frozen_lump. They survive only at the original cadence 10
    (they do survive tracker x0.8/x1.2). **SUSTAINED — 0/2 survive the preregistered cadence sensitivity.**

O4. **Weak placebo?** Not the issue here: placebo_failure = 0/46 and occupancy_alias = 0/46. The positive did not
    die from a weak control; it died from non-replication, frozen lumps, and cadence dependence. **NOT the cause.**

O5. **Denominator inflation.** Every reported fraction has an explicit denominator: N_POST = actual post-
    intervention snapshot count (15 at cadence 10; 30 at cadence 5; 8 at cadence 20 — 150 is not divisible by 20,
    and this is stated rather than silently rounded). A formula-based denominator assertion was found and fixed
    before reporting. **ADDRESSED.**

O6. **Enrollment bias.** Enrollment (first post-warmup structure of size >= 3*min_cells) is frozen from EXP-FL-02
    and does not require probe-positivity at t*, so it is if anything GENEROUS to the hypothesis. 46/48 enrolled,
    2 censored (reported). Enrollment did not manufacture the negative. **NOT a rescue.**

O7. **Displacement into empty space.** PERTURBED moves mass into a region that may be emptier than the origin,
    which would FAVOUR survival of the lump. This makes the negative stronger, not weaker. **NOT a rescue.**

## Verdict
Every route by which the EXP-FL-02 positive could have been real is closed. The positive is an artefact of a
single causal seed plus a missing continued-turnover criterion plus cadence dependence. **The result is withdrawn.
No mechanism, threshold, metric or law was changed to reach this conclusion.**

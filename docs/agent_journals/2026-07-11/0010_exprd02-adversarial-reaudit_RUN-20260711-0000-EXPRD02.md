# EXP-RD-02 — Adversarial independent re-audit (hostile reviewer pass)

Model claude-opus-4-8. Target: the 9 OPEN screening-permitted laws and the 6 apparently-audited causal units.

## Objections

O1. **Base rate.** AUDITED = 6/90 enrolled = 6.7% [Wilson 95% CI 3.1%, 13.8%] across 10 unseen causal seeds per
    law. Five of nine laws are 0/10. This is a weak rate on its own, before any further filter. **SUSTAINED.**

O2. **Displacement destroys the structure.** destroyed = 75/90 (83%). The overwhelming reality is that displacing
    an open-RD dissipative structure off its site destroys it: the organization is NOT carried by its material.
    **SUSTAINED — this is the dominant finding.**

O3. **Placebo and occupancy.** 6 placebo_failure and 3 occupancy_alias units (law 11) are exactly the alias modes
    the audit exists to catch, and they were caught. Law 11 additionally shows heavy fragmentation (27 splits,
    20 ambiguous, 16.5 entities/snapshot) and laws 5, 11, 14 are stationary (occupancy-suspect). **SUSTAINED.**

O4. **Observer fragility.** 0/6 audited units survive the preregistered cadence/tracker sensitivity. **SUSTAINED.**

O5. **SELF-CRITICISM OF MY OWN SENSITIVITY TEST (must be stated, NOT used as a rescue).** My cadence perturbation
    also shifts the ENROLLMENT time (t* = WARMUP_SNAP * cadence), so changing cadence enrols a DIFFERENT structure
    at a DIFFERENT time. It therefore conflates an observer change with an enrollment change and is a stronger
    perturbation than a pure observer test. Notably, all 6 units survive BOTH pure tracker perturbations
    (x0.8, x1.2), and 2 of 6 survive cadence 100 while failing cadence 25.
    **This is a design weakness in my sensitivity check and I flag it rather than exploit it. It does NOT rescue
    the result:** the base rate (6/90), the 83% destruction rate, and the placebo/occupancy failures already fail
    to support any claim. The correct remedy is a FUTURE, pre-declared sensitivity design that perturbs the
    observer WITHOUT moving the enrollment point — not a re-interpretation of this run.

O6. **Was the hold-out too easy?** 9/9 laws survived (many 5/5), i.e. the screening signal RECURS robustly on
    unseen seeds. Recurrence is real; it is simply not evidence of constituent-carried individuality. This is the
    clearest demonstration in the project that Level 3 (recurrence) and Level 5 (causal re-establishment) are
    genuinely different levels. **NOT a rescue; a clarification.**

## Verdict
No route by which the EXP-RD-01 screening signal constitutes constituent-carried, continued-turnover individuality
survives. The result is NEGATIVE. Nothing is promoted. No threshold, metric, mechanism or law was changed.

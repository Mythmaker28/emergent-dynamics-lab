# CLAIM SCOPE — EXP-GT-CONTINUOUS-FINGERPRINT-00

## The claim that was tested

> A **fixed** continuous-response fingerprint can remain **stable** across repeated observations and across
> behaviourally equivalent continuous systems, while **separating** genuinely different accessible dynamics or
> hidden state, under a **declared noise model** and a **fixed intervention repertoire**.

## The claim is NOT ESTABLISHED. The instrument FAILED prospectively and is RETIRED.

It inherits **nothing** from `EXP-GT-FINGERPRINT-00`. The Boolean prospective PASS was not transferable, was not
transferred, and remains confined to **binary observables in a spatial Boolean substrate**.

## Explicitly outside the claim — and still outside it

identity · individuality · lineage · life · agency · selfhood · consciousness · minimal architecture · universal
causality · **droplet transfer** · substrate-independent equivalence · successful material-turnover detection ·
**partition robustness**.

## The fingerprint never says SAME

Verdicts are `INDISTINGUISHABLE_UNDER_REPERTOIRE` · `DIFFERENT` · `INDETERMINATE`. Two systems that no admissible
probe can separate form an **equivalence class**, reported as `EQUIVALENCE_CLASS_ONLY`. Calling them one
individual is an assertion the measurement does not license, and this programme exists because that assertion is
easy to make and hard to retract.

## What the failed instrument nevertheless demonstrated (development + prospective, but NOT a qualification)

These are **observations**, not a certification. The instrument failed its gate and cannot certify anything.

* A continuous response representation can be **exactly invariant** to an affine change of readout units while
  remaining **exactly sensitive** to a change of internal gain — by standardizing to the channel's **noise floor**
  rather than to the signal. Prospective unit-invariance deviation: **0.0**.
* Under a limited repertoire, a genuine structural difference can be **bit-for-bit invisible** (privileged residual
  **0.0**), and this is reportable as a **repertoire-relative equivalence class** rather than as identity.
* Hidden state is detectable **exactly insofar as it changes the causal response**. A hidden state that only added
  a constant to the output would be absorbed by the readout offset and is **correctly unidentifiable**.

## The load-bearing limits, declared

* **Absolute gain is not identifiable when both the output scale and the noise scale are free.** The noise scale is
  therefore **declared, not inferred**, and comparisons across channels of different intrinsic noise are
  **refused**.
* **Gain differences below ~4% are not resolvable**, bounded by the precision of the noise-scale estimate.
* **Partition dependence was never exercised** — `ctrans` has one declared object and no ambiguous boundary. It
  remains **unresolved**.
* The component library is the boundary: what could have been qualified is a fingerprint for **continuous responses
  of systems drawn from this library** — not continuous systems in general, and **not droplets**.

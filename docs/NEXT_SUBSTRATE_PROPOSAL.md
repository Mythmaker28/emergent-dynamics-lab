# Next Substrate — Proposal (candidates, not an assumption)

Context: Particle Dynamics retired for the persistent-individuality question (D-021, kill-switch review). The next
substrate must be evaluated under the SAME falsifiable protocol, not adopted on faith.

## Selection criteria (frozen protocol transfer)
A candidate substrate should offer: (1) genuine constituent turnover with a definable material-retention M and an
ID-independent phenotype P kept separate (no composite); (2) a detector/tracker that does not gate on P or M;
(3) an exact NEUTRAL LIMIT or a CORE-equivalent control; (4) live static-flux / look-alike / occupancy nulls;
(5) a same-state matched-branch causal intervention (displacement/perturbation) to reject occupancy/look-alike;
(6) deterministic, reproducible, second-path-verifiable dynamics.

## Candidate A — Flow-Lenia (primary candidate, to be tested, not assumed)
Flow-Lenia is a mass-conservative continuous cellular automaton in which localized patterns ("creatures") persist
and move while the conserved "mass" flows through them — a natural analogue of persistent organization under
constituent turnover. Fit to criteria: strong on (1) (mass flux gives principled turnover), (2)/(3) (a no-growth /
diffusive parameter regime can serve as the neutral control), (6) (deterministic PDE update). Open work required
BEFORE any claim: define M on the conserved mass/tracer field, port the ID-independent phenotype P, build a
detector/tracker on the continuous field, and adapt the same-state causal intervention (displace a creature's mass
off-site). Risk: continuous fields make "constituent identity" a tracer-labeling choice — must be passive and
non-dynamical, exactly as in EXP-REF-01.

## Candidate B — Reaction-diffusion / Gray-Scott dissipative structures
Well-understood dissipative structures (spots, self-replicating patterns) with a clear neutral (homogeneous)
limit; weaker on discrete constituent turnover unless a passive tracer field is added.

## Candidate C — SPH / active-matter with explicit mass exchange
Particle-based but with continuous mass exchange, keeping the existing detector/tracker/P/M nearly intact; risk of
re-importing the Particle Dynamics occupancy alias.

## Recommendation
Evaluate **Flow-Lenia** first as Candidate A, under a preregistered EXP_FL_00 that (i) ports P/M/detector/tracker
with a passive tracer field, (ii) establishes a neutral control, (iii) runs the measurement positive control
(EXP-REF-01 analogue) on the new stack BEFORE any law search, and only then screens. Adopt it only if it passes
the same gates Particle Dynamics was held to. This is a user decision point: confirm Flow-Lenia (or pick another
candidate) before implementation begins.

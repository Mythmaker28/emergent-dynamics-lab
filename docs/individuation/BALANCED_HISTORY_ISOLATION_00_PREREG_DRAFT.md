# BALANCED-HISTORY-ISOLATION-00 — prospective preregistration draft

**Status:** unsealed draft; blocked pending DEV results and human review.

**Prospective seeds:** not inspected, selected or reserved.

**Prospective execution:** not authorized.

This document is a handoff scaffold, not a preregistration and not permission to run. The scientific question is
whether an isolated complete core can causally express temporal order at exactly matched cumulative dose after deep
material turnover, with cumulative dose serving as the predeclared positive control.

Any future preregistration must carry forward without outcome-driven changes:

- the four fixed histories and 60 + 60 consecutive episode timing;
- eligibility before deterministic Latin-square allocation;
- four-target complete-block analysis and original-world statistical unit;
- condition-specific intention-to-treat survival reporting;
- `M <= 0.25`, longitudinal tracking, fusion/identity/coverage gates and no largest-component reselection;
- untouched complete core, qualified two-cell no-swap clamp, and history-independent `up_ref = 0` primary;
- same-seed/same-time no-history on-manifold boundary reference, unless a second reference is independently justified
  before any prospective outcome is available;
- frozen 40-step integrated tracked uptake, with fixed-mask convergence control;
- within-world dose, order and interaction contrasts at the protocol's conventional scaling;
- the frozen first-stage quantities and orientations;
- `lam_plus = 0` as secondary mediation rather than a validity gate;
- no decoder, feature, boundary reference or horizon selected from prospective outcomes.

The DEV report must determine whether feasibility, first-stage, manipulation and interpretability gates justify
filling in a prospective world count. Any power calculation must use original-world DEV contrasts, account for the
complete-block rate without treating target slots as replicates, state its uncertainty, and be reviewed by Tommy.

Candidate advancement requires all protocol gates and a scientifically interpretable order test. A DEV
`DOSE_ONLY`, `STOP`, `DEV-FEASIBILITY-FAIL`, `MANIPULATION_INVALID`, `NO_LOCAL_TRANSPORT` or unresolved result does
not authorize a prospective family. Even `PREREG-CANDIDATE-GO` requires a separate human-approved frozen
preregistration and namespace audit.

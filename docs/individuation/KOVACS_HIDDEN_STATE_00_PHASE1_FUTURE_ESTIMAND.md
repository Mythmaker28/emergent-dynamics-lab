# KOVACS-HIDDEN-STATE-00 — Phase-1 future primary estimand (T6)

**Trigger status: NOT TRIGGERED.** Task 6 specifies a future primary excursion endpoint *only if*
`STRONG_KOVACS_FEASIBLE` is reached. Phase 1 reached `SCALAR_ONLY_FEASIBLE` (full overt-panel coincidence
`0/17`), so **no future primary estimand is specified or authorised**. This document records that
non-triggering and, for completeness only, the shape such an estimand would take — explicitly **not**
authorised and **not** to be executed.

## Why not triggered

`STRONG_KOVACS_FEASIBLE` requires a full overt-panel coincidence under a mechanically-defensible
tolerance. The natural dose-matched coincidence leaves systematic overt residuals (`0/17` qualified), so
the precondition fails. No excursion endpoint may be attached to a design whose macrostate coincidence is
not established.

## Conditional shape (for completeness; not authorised, not executed)

Were a future design to achieve a genuine full-panel coincidence, the single frozen primary endpoint
would be: the signed (and absolute) area of the post-release excursion of the **same matched coordinate**
over **one frozen horizon**, aggregated once per **original source world**, with an exact
**direct-relaxation reference** sham and an **identical-history** sham (both ~0), and **no** time-point,
variable, or horizon selection. The frozen classifications would be:

| class | meaning |
|---|---|
| KOVACS_HIDDEN_STATE_SUPPORTED | full-panel coincidence valid; shams ~0; signed excursion nonzero in a frozen direction across worlds |
| OPPOSITE_SIGN_MEMORY | coincidence valid; excursion nonzero but with the opposite sign to the frozen expectation |
| NO_HIDDEN_STATE_ESTABLISHED | coincidence valid; excursion indistinguishable from the shams (bounded null; never "no hidden state") |
| MACROSTATE_MISMATCH | full overt panel not coincident at tolerance (the Phase-1 outcome) |
| MANIPULATION_INVALID | matching/release depends on future info, surgery, regression, or uncontrolled clock time |
| UNRESOLVED | validity holds but controls conflict or power is insufficient |

**Equivalence is never claimed without a pre-outcome scientific margin.** Because Phase 1 could not
establish that margin (the overt residual is systematic and cannot be shown negligible without excursion
sizes), this estimand remains dormant. Any activation requires a new human-reviewed design that first
achieves a full-panel coincidence.

## 1. What Phase 1 tested and found

Phase 0 showed core-region mass is crossable, but a mass match leaves large overt differences (area,
shape, memory), so a positive excursion after scalar mass matching would establish only "core-region mass
is insufficient" — a claim already implied by the accepted physical-carryover result, hence too weak to
justify a prospective family. Phase 1 therefore sought a stronger **natural macrostate-coincidence**: two
exact-clone histories brought to coincidence on a compact overt physical panel (mass, area, 2nd moment,
centroid, core energy/nutrient, collar) without surgery, regression, transplantation, or outcome tuning.

Finding (17 complete DEV worlds, dose-matched spike-vs-sustained, common-clock coincidence, **no excursion
computed**): the overt panel matches to only ~1–2 % relative, but the residual is **systematic** and above
every mechanical tolerance (identical-history sham 0; 3σ natural repeatability) — **0/17** worlds pass the
full panel. A consistent slow-memory residual remains (m2, m_minus 17/17), but per the value gate that is
the *limitation*, not positive evidence. Verdict: `SCALAR_ONLY_FEASIBLE → STOP_PROSPECTIVE`.

## 2. Why no prospective family is preregistered

- The coincidence-qualification rate is **0** at a defensible tolerance, so a STRONG prospective is
  infeasible on attrition alone (power report).
- The overt residual cannot be shown negligible relative to the hidden residual **without** excursion
  sizes, which are forbidden — so a pre-outcome equivalence margin cannot be established.
- The only overt-coincident natural axis (the parent order axis) has an even weaker hidden margin and is
  the stopped `NO_MEMORY_FIRST_STAGE` line; reopening it is not justified and out of scope.

**before** any excursion. Only then would the dormant future estimand

## Formal definition

Let `S_t` be the complete physical state and let `D(S_t)` be a fixed instantaneous detector that uses current
physical fields only. It returns zero or more candidate operational entities `O_t=(Omega_t,B_t)`, where `Omega_t`
is a material support and `B_t` is a set of physical boundary faces. Let `Lambda_t` be a set-valued longitudinal
association relation used only for measurement. Let `Q` be a declared native response, `H` a frozen horizon, and
`I` a precompiled intervention plan.

An operational entity is an **interventionally addressable individual at `(Q,H,I)`** only if every property below
holds:

1. **Longitudinal persistence.** ID-independent operational organization remains associated through the declared
   horizon while material can cross the boundary. Persistence is not inferred from appearance alone and does not
   force a one-to-one lineage through split or merge.
2. **Localized addressability.** A state-preserving intervention changes only a declared pre-existing causal edge
   on a fixed cell or physical-face set. A local response is not enough; confinement, shams, and negative regions
   must exclude broad manipulation.
3. **Independent composition.** A-only, B-only, and A+B plans compile from the same state. `I_A o I_B` and
   `I_B o I_A` have identical canonical bytes, direct assignments, and outputs through the first reachable
   arithmetic operation.
4. **State-preserving onset.** Immediately after intervention assignment and before the next right-hand-side
   evaluation, every physical state byte, clock, geometry, boundary state, source state, and common probe equals
   the open clone. Only an out-of-state coefficient plan differs.
5. **Conservation and work closure.** Every extensive update decomposes into oriented face fluxes, declared local
   source/sink terms, reservoirs, and controller/boundary work. Paired face contributions cancel exactly within an
   operation-derived numerical bound. No manipulation residual is hidden in a response.
6. **Open-mode identity.** The neutral/open plan takes the unmodified base update path and is byte-identical for
   arbitrary synthetic states; forced-neutral active paths must also satisfy their separately frozen identity.
7. **Sufficient intervention rank.** Every causal taxonomy is supported by a full-rank design made of valid
   interventions, not merely four arm labels. For the later declared `L x E` assay, rank must be 4.
8. **Tracker-independent definition.** `D` and physics use neither `Lambda`, diagnostic IDs, future response,
   phenotype `P`, nor material retention `M`. Intervention supports are frozen at `t0` and never follow a track.

These conditions are necessary, not jointly sufficient for an unrestricted concept of individuality. Failure of
any condition falsifies the declared causally addressable-individual claim. Passing them permits only the stated
response, scale, channels, and horizon; it does not establish metaphysical identity, ownership, reproduction, or
life.

### A. Phase-field droplets with a dynamical semipermeable interface

A conservative Cahn–Hilliard form can make interface location emergent:

```text
partial_t phi = div(M(phi) grad mu)
mu = f'(phi) - kappa lap(phi)
partial_t c = -div(J_c) + R(phi,c)
J_c = -D(phi,abs(grad phi)) grad c
```

Material turnover and split/merge are possible, and grid infrastructure is familiar. The difficulty is causal
addressability: `mu` includes a high-order stencil, `D` is a nonlinear function of a diffuse interface, and an
entity boundary requires a threshold or contour choice. An E gate on interface mobility can be conservative, but
it can also turn an order-parameter contour into the compartment whose autonomy is then “discovered.” Complete raw
face-parent attribution and a tracker-free fixed-face support are possible only after a substantial finite-volume
qualification. This is not the smallest *causally legible* substrate even if it uses few field arrays.

### B. Particle aggregates with conservative contact-edge fluxes

For a physical contact edge `(i,j)`, a resource or cargo flux

```text
J_ij^k = kappa_ij^k (g_i^k - g_j^k) = -J_ji^k
```

is naturally modular and conservative. This family is closest to the existing `LawSpec`: current mechanics can be
retained as a composed, unchanged particle-force law while a new exchange spec governs contacts. Material exchange,
turnover, split, and merge can all occur.

The fatal Phase-0 weakness is not conservation but localization. Without a separate physical membrane state, the
“boundary” is the detector’s cut of a contact graph. A short-lived aggregate edge can be called internal or external
only relative to a chosen component. Keeping an intervention attached to moving A or B then tends to require
particle IDs, persistent treatment flags, or tracker feedback. A fixed spatial intervention avoids IDs but no longer
targets the same aggregate once it moves. The family may remain useful for other questions; it is not selected for
the smallest tracker-independent addressability program.

### C. Lattice matter with emergent membrane bonds

Candidate C puts the physical coupling on fixed lattice faces while leaving closure and entities emergent. Every
undirected nearest-neighbour face has the same dynamical bond/permeability variable. A detector may later recognize
a closed high-bond cut-set, but no such component is visible to the equations. A face can remain open, form part of
an open sheet, percolate, dissolve, or participate in a closing, splitting, or fusing boundary.

This is the strongest falsifiable option: the face ledger is exact even when no entity exists; an unsuccessful law
can fail by never closing a boundary; and a static impermeable shell with no turnover fails rather than being
rescued as individuality.

## Minimal selected equations

Let `G=(V,E)` be a periodic square lattice with one stored orientation per undirected nearest-neighbour face. Before

`b` is a physical structural order parameter, not an experience-dependent state. It cannot be used as the later

### Matter motion

Define a local affinity with no global normalization:

```text
A_x = theta_m sum_{y~x} w_xy m_y + theta_n n_x.
```

For each orientation of face `e={x,y}`:

```text
F^m_{x->y} = kappa_m (1-b_e) m_x (1-m_y/m_max) exp[(A_y-A_x)/2]
J^m_{xy}   = F^m_{x->y} - F^m_{y->x} = -J^m_{yx}
m_x'       = m_x - dt sum_{y~x} J^m_{xy}.
```

The two nonnegative directed propensities and their net are raw observables. Numerical stabilization may not replace
them by an inseparable destination formula. Before implementation, Stage A must prove `0 <= m_x' <= m_max` on a
declared compact admissible-state domain using a CFL bound derived from the maximum outgoing and incoming directed
propensities, or freeze a deterministic input-derived substep/antisymmetric face-limiter rule. Every coefficient
and residual must be logged per face, the same coefficient must multiply both endpoint contributions, and the
scalar reference must use the same declared operation order. Output-selected clipping is a kill switch.

### Environmental resource and native response

```text
J^n_{xy} = D_n p_n(b_e) (n_x-n_y) = -J^n_{yx},  0 <= p_n(b) <= 1
tilde n_x = n_x - dt sum_{y~x} J^n_{xy}
                  + dt [S_n(x)-D_n_local(x)-R_native(m_x,n_x)]
n_x' = tilde n_x - (epsilon_b/2) sum_{e incident x} [delta b_e]_+.
```

`R_native` is an ordinary resource conversion/uptake path present before any history field. The scientific response
`Q` must be a separately frozen substrate-native downstream behavior observable from the ordinary state trajectory,
not the directly gated `h -> R_native` term and not the resource crossing flux multiplied by `q_E`. Those two
quantities are raw manipulation/compliance and conservation diagnostics only. No bond-specific or
history-specific reporter is added. Any external reservoir is an explicit local source `S_n`, never a world mean.
The closed Stage-A conservation fixtures set declared sources and sinks to zero rather than hiding them.

Stage A must also prove `n_x' >= 0` on the declared admissible domain. The required CFL inequality accounts jointly
for outward face transport, `D_n_local`, `R_native`, bond-formation fuel below, and declared input `S_n`. If the
single-step inequality is not uniform, a deterministic predeclared substep schedule computed from common input
state—not arm output—must be used for every clone. A negative-value clamp or arm-specific step count is forbidden.

### Bond dynamics

Let `Psi_xy` be a symmetric nonnegative function of endpoint and fixed-neighbour physical state only. It may detect
local occupancy contrast or membrane-forming chemistry, but it may not depend on a component, contour closure,
inside/outside class, entity count, tracker, or diagnostic ID. Let `T_xy` be a local tension/curvature proxy with the
same restriction:

```text
r_on  = k_on Psi_xy
r_off = k_off + k_tension T_xy
b_eq  = r_on/(r_on+r_off)
b_e'  = b_eq + (b_e-b_eq) exp[-(r_on+r_off) dt].
```

When both rates vanish, `b_e'=b_e`. This exact local relaxation keeps `b` in `[0,1]` without output-selected
clipping. Let `delta b_e=b_e'-b_e` and let one unit of `b` store `epsilon_b` units of the already-present resource
energy. Formation withdraws `epsilon_b [delta b_e]_+/2` from each endpoint resource; dissociation releases
`Q_e^bond=epsilon_b[-delta b_e]_+` to an explicitly logged heat/dissipation sink. If endpoint resource is
insufficient, Stage A must either prove the state inadmissible under the frozen CFL domain or apply one
predeclared symmetric formation limiter derived from the common input. The raw proposed increment, limiter, actual
increment, endpoint withdrawals, and heat are all logged. This makes formation fuel finite and observed; it is not
an unmeasured controller.

### Conservation and work identities

For every closed update:

```text
sum_x (m_x'-m_x) = 0
E_b = epsilon_b sum_e b_e
Delta [sum_x n_x + E_b]
    + dt sum_x [D_n_local+R_native] + sum_e Q_e^bond
    = dt sum_x S_n
```

The second resource line applies the half-face bond-formation withdrawals after transport/reaction. Expanding the
identity face by face gives
`Delta E_b = sum_e epsilon_b[delta b_e]_+ - sum_e Q_e^bond`; the first term is exactly the summed endpoint fuel
withdrawal. No bond energy, fuel, heat, source, or controller term is left implicit.

For any frozen spatial support `Omega`:

```text
Delta M_Omega = -dt sum_{e in boundary(Omega)} s(Omega,e) J^m_e
                + declared sources - declared sinks.
```

Every active face contributes `-dt J_e` to one endpoint and `+dt J_e` to the other. Their global sum must be zero
within a tolerance derived from the exact floating-point operation count. Per-face open/active fluxes, positivity
limiter, source, sink, reservoir, bond-energy change, and controller switching work are logged separately.
Assigning a coefficient plan changes no state byte and creates no state-energy jump at onset. It does not exempt the
subsequent controlled flux from the ledger.

or updated from the detector or tracker. A target that moves outside its fixed causal support yields an estimand
limit or missing outcome, not permission to follow it with an identity label.

### Environmental port

On fixed physical high-bond faces, `q_E` multiplies both orientations of one declared pre-existing resource flux:

```text
F^{n,q}_{x->y} = q_E(e) F^n_{x->y}
F^{n,q}_{y->x} = q_E(e) F^n_{y->x}
J^{n,q}_{xy}   = q_E(e) J^n_{xy} = -J^{n,q}_{yx}.
```

The two endpoints receive equal-and-opposite updates. The gate does not replace the environment, freeze a state,
create a reservoir, or close matter/cargo flux. `E0` means only unavailability of the declared boundary-resource
channel over the declared faces and window; it is never “absence of environment” or proof of natural autonomy.

The environmental gate is not credited if it acts on an ordinary detector graph cut rather than an existing
physical bond face, if it gates only the target destination, if controller/bond work is absent, or if a different
environment-to-response route is claimed controlled without its own edge inventory.

The open/active crossing flux is a manipulation and conservation diagnostic, never the primary `Q`. Otherwise a

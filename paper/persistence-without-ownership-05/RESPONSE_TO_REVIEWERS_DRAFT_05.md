# Draft response to hostile reviewers

This response anticipates external review. Line numbers should be added after journal formatting.

## Response to Reviewer 1

**Ownership is author-defined and the failed contrasts are underpowered.** We agree that ownership is operational rather than metaphysical. We now define it before presenting results as a conservative sufficiency conjunction: target-local own-history information must exceed every frozen alternative scope with a positive lower paired interval, in addition to permutation significance and causal expression. The manuscript does not infer equality from the failed E and B comparisons. It reports their positive point estimates and wide intervals in the abstract, Results, Discussion, Limitations, Figure 6, and the supplement. The claim is “not established as locally owned,” not “proven nonlocal.” The phrase “falsified within the tested criteria” applies only to the proposition that causal persistence is sufficient for individual memory under this preregistered definition.

**A larger study could pass.** Correct. The 03G result is a finite counterexample to sufficiency in this architecture and decision rule, not a universal impossibility theorem. We explicitly state that another architecture, representation, or larger predeclared experiment could produce a different gate vector. We did not add reserve worlds after the minimum was met because doing so would violate the sealed one-shot plan.

**Twenty-one of fifty worlds are selected.** The estimand is explicitly conditional on frozen feasibility, tracking, assay, and deep-turnover validity. We added the exact 50→21 flow and no-reserve fact to the abstract, Methods, Figure 4, supplement, numerical ledger, and limitations. The 29 invalid worlds are retained in the raw registry and certificate; they were not excluded by outcome. A post-hoc intention-to-analyze sensitivity would require assigning outcomes to worlds in which the target or assay is undefined and was not performed for this synthesis.

**Decoder scopes are arbitrary.** They are model-dependent, but not chosen after the outcome. The paper now calls them finite access scopes and avoids treating `DISTRIBUTED_ENV=false` as environmental absence. The experiment can tell that L did not satisfy the frozen exclusivity criterion and cannot tell which unmeasured representation, if any, owns the state. That unresolved residue is central to Outcome B.

**The causal readout is engineered.** Agreed. The title and central claim use “engineered state,” and the abstract and discussion state that the feeding channel is directly coupled by construction. The value of the causal gate is logical separation: even a clearly causal, turnover-surviving state can fail ownership. We do not claim emergent behavior.

**The correction history looks unstable.** We retain it because suppressing it would overstate confidence. It explains why the present protocol uses original-world grouping, explicit tracking, non-fusing confirmation, sealed scopes, and raw-only reproduction. Readers may judge whether those remedies are adequate.

We do not adopt the proposed “inconclusive” title because the preregistered sufficiency gate did return a determinate failure and the decision tree returned Outcome B. We have, however, consistently phrased the result as failure to establish local ownership, never proof of no ownership.

## Response to Reviewer 2

**Passive copying and direct wiring make the result expected.** This is a fair mechanistic description and is now stated explicitly in the abstract interpretation, operational definitions, model section, discussion, limitations, and public summary. The result is useful precisely because persistence and causality can look compelling while remaining insufficient. We do not describe active reconstruction.

**Biological vocabulary is inflated.** We use “simulated droplets” as a geometrical/model label and explicitly deny that they are cells or protocells. Related work provides conceptual context, not validation by analogy. “Deep turnover,” “individual,” “history,” and “memory” each receive operational definitions. The conclusion is restricted to C1c and the tested criterion.

**The seal is software ceremony.** The seal does not establish truth. It establishes temporal provenance: the scopes, statistics, decision rule, and seed family existed before the one-shot run. We added a sentence to the supplement and response material distinguishing integrity controls from scientific validity.

**V4.1 should be removed.** We retain V4.1 as corrected history, not primary support. The manuscript names all three world-fold values, withdraws the former certification, states that h2 is not established, and notes the fusion-reconstruction limitation. Removing it would hide why the turnover protocol was necessary. Figure 1 and the claim ledger mark it as historical and qualified.

**Reframe as a methods paper.** We agree substantially. The manuscript’s main contribution is the falsification ladder and worked prospective dissociation. It does not assert a biological discovery. The empirical component supplies the nontrivial gate combination rather than a claim that the droplets are individuals.

## Response to Reviewer 3

**Exchangeability and validity filtering.** Original-world grouping prevents the demonstrated row/history leakage but does not guarantee all modeling assumptions. We state this limitation. The Student intervals describe variation over valid original-world fold scores; they are not population-wide guarantees. The estimand is conditional on validity.

**No equivalence margin.** Correct. We make no equivalence or absence claim. A lower interval crossing zero fails the predeclared positive-advantage rule. “Ownership gate failed” means the conjunction was not met. The numerical values remain visible so readers can see the uncertainty.

**Multiplicity and conservative conjunction.** The four-scope rule is a sufficiency test. Its purpose is to prevent one favorable comparison from being promoted to exclusivity. It can have high false-negative probability, which is why our negative class is “falsified within the tested criteria,” not a universal claim. The permutation gate and causal gate are reported separately rather than collapsed into a composite score.

**Raw-only match is not replication.** Agreed. We consistently call it an independent implementation or raw-only reproduction, not an independent experiment. The 9,357 count describes recursive comparison coverage, not sample size; sample size remains 21 original worlds.

**Hashes and seals do not establish validity.** Agreed. They bind provenance and exact bytes. The correction history shows that bound artifacts can still contain conceptual errors. Scientific claims depend on the design and evidence, not the digest itself.

**Human metadata are blank.** The package is ready for external scientific review, not submission. The cover letter and manuscript mark author, affiliation, ORCID, funding, conflicts, and journal fields for human completion. No automated submission or contact is authorized.

**Figures should expose scores and gate authorship.** Figure 5 and Figure 6 show estimates and uncertainty, while the machine-readable derived JSON contains every fold score. The decision figure labels the rule as frozen/preregistered, and its caption identifies the source decision tree. A journal revision could add a supplementary world-score panel, but generating a new figure is not necessary to interpret the present package and no new analysis was authorized in this synthesis.

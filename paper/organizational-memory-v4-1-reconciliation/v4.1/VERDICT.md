# Final verdict

**MAJOR REPAIR STILL REQUIRED**

The V4.1 reconciliation itself is complete and reproducible without new
simulation, but the underlying evidence package cannot support the former
external-review claim because:

1. only three original worlds support the longitudinal headline;
2. the V4 confidence interval is leakage-affected and is withdrawn;
3. the corrected lower uncertainty bound does not clear the 0.50 threshold;
4. event-level tracker and fusion decisions are not committed and cannot be
   independently reconstructed;
5. the globally imposed history does not distinguish global informational
   content from local storage.

No new simulation is authorized by this verdict. It identifies what the
existing artifacts can and cannot support.

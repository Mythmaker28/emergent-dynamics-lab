## MANDATORY CAUTION — the Flow-Lenia precedent
EXP-FL-02 produced exactly this shape of result (24 permitted laws, 0 in the control) and it DIED under
replication: the audited rate collapsed to 2/46 on unseen seeds, 65% of "successes" were FROZEN LUMPS (structures
that re-establish then STOP turning over), and the last two were cadence artefacts (D-027). **A screening
permission in this project has, historically, been worth nothing.**
Live falsifiers here: OPEN shows ~3x the track count of CLOSED (21.3 vs 6.7) -- a fragmentation / churn signature
(F2); and the imposed-pattern/occupancy alias is untested at this stage. No threshold was changed, no composite
formed, no law selected visually.

## DECISION
**EXP-RD-01 SCREEN = CANDIDATES (9 permitted OPEN laws). NOTHING IS PROMOTED.** The frozen downstream chain is now
mandatory and must be run in order: fresh-seed hold-out (>=2/5 unseen seeds) -> direct alias audit INCLUDING the
imposed-pattern/occupancy check -> same-state CONTROL/SHAM/PERTURBED/PLACEBO causal intervention **WITH the
continued-turnover requirement** -> observer (cadence/tracker) sensitivity -> adversarial re-audit. Report explicit
denominators, censored seeds, effect sizes and Wilson CIs.
